# Plantex Sales Dashboard 🌿

A powerful, entirely Server-Side Rendered (SSR) analytics platform designed for detailed multi-metric visualization, and fast asynchronous execution. Plantex provides specialized data aggregations for Business, CEO, and Category Manager roles in real time!

## 🚀 Features

- **Robust Asynchronous Pipelines:** Replenishment and Master Report generation utilizes **Celery** to handle massive Pandas datasets in the background. This ensures that long-running calculations (validations and merges) never block the UI or time out, even when processing hundreds of thousands of rows.
- **Real-Time Progress & Recovery:** File parsing and generation tasks provide live state tracking. The system utilizes **LocalStorage** and constant polling to ensure that if you navigate away from a dashboard or reload the page, you instantly "re-attach" to any ongoing background jobs without losing progress.
- **Multi-Platform Support:** Seamlessly process complex sales reports, PCA, PLA, and inventory matrices from both **Amazon** and **Flipkart** environments without conflict to provide holistic brand metrics.
- **Server-Side Rendered (SSR):** Context injection using Pandas and NumPy natively into Django templates keeping standard `Business Report`, `CEO Report`, and `Category Report` templates free of cluttered vanilla JavaScript processing logic!
- **Sleek Graphics Engine:** Fluid graph generations implemented purely utilizing Chart.js driven by localized REST contexts natively generated by the SSR engine.
- **Live Data Exports:** Hit **Download CSV** or **Download Excel** directly from your dashboards to export pre-calculated, merged ASIN arrays (along with ROAS, TACoS, and Net Profit columns) that strictly respect your currently active UI filters. Excel exports even feature a dedicated Annexure sheet defining all generated formulas.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Backend:** Django, Django REST Framework, Django Channels, Pandas, NumPy, Celery
- **Broker & Results:** Redis Server
- **Frontend Layer:** HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS (ES6+), Chart.js

## ⚙️ Installation & Usage

### 1. Prerequisites 

Make sure the following dependencies are installed globally on your machine:
- `python 3.x`
- `redis-server` (Running on default port `6379`)

### 2. Setup

Clone the project locally and jump into root directory. Setup your environment and load Django packages inside `pltx_dashboard`:
```bash
python3 -m venv venv
source venv/bin/activate
pip install -r requirements.txt
```

Run database migrations natively via the ORM:
```bash
python manage.py makemigrations
python manage.py migrate
```

### 3. Spin Up Server

To witness the robust power of the WebSocket progress bars alongside the file ingestion engine, simply spin up Daphne:

**Start Django Channels server using Daphne:**
```bash
cd pltx_dashboard
daphne pltx_dashboard.asgi:application --port 8000
```

**Start the Celery worker (Required for Replenishment):**
In a separate terminal window, run:
```bash
cd pltx_dashboard
celery -A pltx_dashboard worker -l info
```
> Note: While Daphne handles the WebSockets and HTTP traffic, Celery is required to execute the intensive Replenishment and Master Report generation tasks in the background.
Navigate to `http://127.0.0.1:8000/` and test real-time dashboard analytics out right now!

## 📊 Understanding the Analytics (Layman's Guide)

The Plantex Dashboard simplifies complex e-commerce data into actionable insights. Here is a breakdown of the key metrics used across our dashboards in simple terms:

### 💰 Financial & Profitability Metrics
- **Total Revenue**: The total amount of money earned from your product sales before any expenses.
  - *Formula:* Sum of all sold product prices.
- **Gross Margin (%)**: Shows what percentage of your revenue is profit after paying for the product's cost (COGS).
  - *Layman:* "For every ₹100 you earn, how much is left after you pay for the product itself?"
  - *Formula:* `((Revenue - Product Cost) / Revenue) * 100`
- **Contribution Margin**: A "Real Profit" indicator that shows how much profit is left after paying for both the product cost and advertising spend.
  - *Layman:* "This is your actual profit margin after the two biggest expenses (Stock + Ads) are cleared."
  - *Formula:* `Gross Margin % - TACOS %`

### 📈 Growth & Performance
- **MOM Growth (Month-over-Month)**: Compares your current performance to the previous period to see if you are growing.
  - *Formula:* `((Current Value - Previous Value) / Previous Value) * 100`
- **AOV (Average Order Value)**: The average amount a customer spends in a single order.
  - *Layman:* "How much, on average, are customers paying per checkout?"
  - *Formula:* `Total Revenue / Total Orders`
- **Conversion Rate (CVR)**: The percentage of people who visited your product page and ended up buying something.
  - *Layman:* "Higher CVR means your listing is convincing and the price is right!"
  - *Formula:* `(Orders / Pageviews) * 100`

### 🎯 Advertising Efficiency
- **TACOS (Total Advertising Cost of Sales)**: Measures how much of your total revenue is eaten up by advertising.
  - *Layman:* "A TACOS of 15% means you spend ₹15 on ads for every ₹100 you make in total sales. Lower is usually better!"
  - *Formula:* `(Total Ad Spend / Total Revenue) * 100`
- **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)**: Measures how many rupees you earn for every ₹1 spent on ads.
  - *Layman:* "If ROAS is 5x, it means your ads are turning ₹1 into ₹5 in sales."
  - *Formula:* `Total Revenue / Total Ad Spend`

### 📦 Inventory Health
- **Days of Cover**: An estimate of how many days your current stock will last based on how fast you are selling.
  - *Layman:* "Used to predict when you will run out of stock so you can re-order in time."
- **OOS (Out of Stock) Impact**: Calculates potential revenue lost because products were unavailable while customers were looking for them.
  - *Layman:* "Tells you exactly how much money you 'left on the table' because you didn't have stock."
